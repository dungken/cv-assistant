# Education Guide

The education section showcases your academic background.

## Placement
- **Recent Grads**: Place it **before** Experience.
- **Experienced Pros**: Place it **after** Experience.

## Format
- **Degree**: Full name (e.g., Bachelor of Science in Computer Science).
- **Institution**: University/College name.
- **Graduation Date**: Month/Year (or expected date).
- **GPA**: Include only if > 3.0/4.0 (or strong for your region).
- **Honors**: Magna Cum Laude, Dean's List, etc.

## Example
> **Bachelor of Science in Computer Science**
> University of Technology | May 2018
> - GPA: 3.8/4.0
> - Relevant Coursework: Data Structures, Algorithms, AI.
