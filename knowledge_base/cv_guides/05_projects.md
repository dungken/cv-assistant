# Projects Guide

Projects demonstrate your practical application of skills, especially for students or career switchers.

## Selection
- Choose 2-3 significant projects.
- Prioritize projects that use technologies relevant to the target job.
- Open Source contributions are highly valued.

## Format
- **Project Name**: Hyperlink to GitHub/Demo if possible.
- **Role**: e.g., "Sole Developer" or "Frontend Lead".
- **Technologies Used**: List them clearly.
- **Description**: 1-2 bullet points on what it does and your specific contribution.

## Example
> **E-commerce Platform** (Link) | _React, Node.js, MongoDB_
> - Built a full-stack dashboard for inventory management.
> - Implemented secure JWT authentication and Stripe payment integration.
