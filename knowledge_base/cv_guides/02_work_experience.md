# Work Experience Guide

The work experience section is the core of your CV.

## Structure
- Reverse chronological order (newest first).
- **Format**: Job Title | Company Name | Dates | Location.

## Writing Bullet Points
- Use **Action Verbs** to start each bullet (e.g., Developed, Managed, Created).
- Focus on **Achievements** rather than just duties.
- **Quantify** your results whenever possible (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%").

## Example
> **Software Engineer | Tech Corp | 2020 - Present**
> - Led the migration of legacy monolith to microservices, reducing deployment time by 40%.
> - Mentored 3 junior developers.
