# Common CV Mistakes Guide

Avoid these common errors to keep your CV in the "Yes" pile.

## Formatting
- **Inconsistent Layout**: Different fonts, bullet styles, or date formats.
- **Cluttered Design**: Lack of white space.
- **Too Long**: Keep it to 1-2 pages maximum.

## Content
- **Typos/Grammar**: Use tools like Grammarly and proofread manually.
- **Personal Details**: Age, religion, marital status (unless required locally).
- **Generic Duties**: Listing responsibilities instead of achievements.
- **Unprofessional Email**: Use `firstname.lastname@gmail.com`.

## Strategy
- **One Size Fits All**: Not tailoring the CV to the job description (ATS failure).
- **Keyword Stuffing**: listing keywords without context.
