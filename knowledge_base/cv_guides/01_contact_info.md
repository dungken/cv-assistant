# Contact Information Guide

Your contact information is the first thing recruiters see. It should be clear and professional.

## What to Include
- **Full Name**: Use your legal name or professional name.
- **Phone Number**: Include country code if applying internationally.
- **Email Address**: Use a professional format (e.g., firstname.lastname@email.com).
- **Location**: City and State/Country is sufficient. Full address is rarely needed.
- **LinkedIn/Portfolio**: Hyperlinked URL to your profile.

## Common Mistakes
- Using unprofessional email aliases (e.g., coolguy123@...).
- Including marital status, religion, or photo (depending on country/region norms).
