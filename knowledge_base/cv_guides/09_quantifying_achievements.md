# Quantifying Achievements Guide

Numbers speak louder than words. Quantifying your impact proves your value.

## Metrics to Use
- **Time**: "Reduced build time by **30%**."
- **Money**: "Saved **$10,000** annually in cloud costs."
- **Volume**: "Handled **500+** requests per second."
- **People**: "Managed a team of **10** engineers."
- **Growth**: "Increased user retention by **15%**."

## Formula
> **Action Verb** + **Task** + **Result (Number)**

> "Optimized SQL queries (Action) for the reporting dashboard (Task), reducing load times from 10s to 2s (Result)."
