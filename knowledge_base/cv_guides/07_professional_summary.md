# Professional Summary Guide

A professional summary is a brief statement at the top of your resume that describes your experience, skills, and achievements.

## When to use
- **Experienced Professionals**: To highlight career highlights.
- **Career Changers**: To connect past experience to new goals.
- **Fresh Graduates**: Use an "Objective" statement instead (focus on what you can offer).

## Format
- 3-5 sentences or bullet points.
- Tailored to the specific job description.

## Example
> "Results-oriented Software Engineer with 5+ years of experience in full-stack development. Proven track record of improving system performance by 40% and leading cross-functional teams. Skilled in Python, React, and Cloud Architecture."
