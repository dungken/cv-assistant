# Action Verbs Guide

Use strong action verbs to start your bullet points. Avoid passive voice or weak words like "Responsible for".

## Leadership
- Led, Managed, Mentored, Chaired, Directed, Supervised, Trained.

## Communication
- Presented, Negotiated, Authored, Collaborated, Persuaded.

## Technical/Creative
- Architected, Engineered, Developed, Designed, Implemented, Automated.

## Achievement
- Achieved, Improved, Increased, Reduced, Saved, Generated, Won.

## Example
- **Weak**: "Responsible for writing code."
- **Strong**: "**Developed** and **deployed** a microservice that **reduced** latency by 20%."
